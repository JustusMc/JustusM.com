Hey there! I’m a Web Developer based in Beautiful British Columbia!

My name is Justus, I am very passionate about programming. I am now 26 years old and I want to pursue a career in programming. I wish to learn as much as I can in my lifetime and even when I am 80 I still want to be learning! I am attracted to programming like an addicting video game that you just can't stop playing. No matter how frustrated or difficult it gets I just want to keep advancing to the next level! I hope to accomplish in my lifetime to even make the Leaderboards!

As a Husband and Father I wish to provide my family the best life possible using my mind and skills in programming. If I don't know it I will learn it. I wish to branch out as much as possible to any area of programming whether it be Software Development, Front-end Development or Full Stack Development. As long as I am coding and working with a team to achieve something greater than ourselves I will feel fulfilled!

It doesn't stop there, I am highly motivated and dedicated and have been pursuing this field for 5 years now. With my 5+years of Technical experience and my 5 years of experience experimenting with Code I believe I would be an amazing developer on a team given the proper training to take my skills and make them professional! I still have a lot to learn and with Programming "the more you know the more you don't know"

I am looking for a company to give me a chance and I won't let them down! I will not rest until I meet my goal!
